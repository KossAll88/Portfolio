About Me

Artist & Illustrator from Ukraine

Hello, I'm Matvii — an artist and illustrator passionate about creating warm, atmospheric and emotionally rich illustrations.

I graduated from art school, where I studied traditional drawing, painting and composition for 5 years. After that, I completed several specialized courses in digital illustration and continued to develop my skills in Procreate, Photoshop and other digital tools.

My works combine classical art education with modern digital techniques. I especially enjoy creating detailed illustrations with a cozy and slightly melancholic atmosphere.
